# Privacy Policy - XHS Tool Pro

## Introduction
XHS Tool Pro is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension handles data.

## Data Collection and Use
XHS Tool Pro **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information** to external servers. All data processing is performed locally within the user's browser.

- **Cookies**: The extension accesses creator.xiaohongshu.com cookies to extract authentication tokens for display to the user. This information is **never** sent to any third party.
- **API Interception (Network Activity)**: The extension hooks into specific Xiaohongshu API calls (posted/user) to capture business data such as 'traffic level' for display on the local page. This data remains on your machine.
- **No Third-Party Analytics**: We do not use any tracking scripts or third-party analytics services.

## Security
We take security seriously. Since no data is transmitted externally, your information is protected by the security of your local environment and browser.

## Contact
For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer via GitHub.
